Introduction
PrepForge AI (Interview Craft) is an AI-powered placement preparation platform designed to simulate real-world interview experiences. It focuses on bridging the gap between conceptual knowledge and practical interview performance, particularly in HR and behavioral rounds.
Unlike traditional preparation tools that provide static questions, PrepForge AI creates a adaptive, and personalized interview environment based on a candidate’s resume, projects.

2. Problem Statement
Students preparing for placements often:
•	Understand technical concepts but struggle to express them clearly in interviews 
•	Face difficulty in HR and behavioral rounds, especially when discussing projects 
•	Practice with generic question banks that lack personalization 
•	Lack exposure to realistic interview pressure and follow-up questioning 
This leads to poor performance despite having sufficient knowledge.

3. Proposed Solution
PrepForge AI addresses these issues by:
•	Analyzing user resumes and GitHub projects 
•	Generating context-aware, role-specific interview questions 
•	Providing structured guidance on how to answer 
The platform transforms preparation from passive learning into an interactive, interview-like experience.
